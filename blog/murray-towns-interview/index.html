HOW LUCKY ARE WE?! A wonderful experience for the Border Barrels lads, as Jodie and Barry from Murray Towns Brewing Co. joined us for a relaxing Sunday afternoon session at The Woolhouse in Albury.
These generous gentlemen supplied us with all the exclusives! Two development beers being the immediate highlight (Jodie is an absolute magician), plus all the exciting details of a future Murray Towns brewpub.
We can already taste A Beer Named Sue, sure to be pouring on opening night. Jodie and Barry also provided us with details of their own personal beer journeys, and what ultimately lead to the creation of Murray Towns (Hint: It almost had a very different, hilarious name).
Make sure to check our their current range of beers, available at select local venues on tap and in cans at local beer dealers.
Thanks again to Barry and Jodie, as well as Gav and the team at The Woolhouse for allowing us to use their wonderful venue for this podcast.

Check the episode out on YouTube, Spotify, or Apple Podcasts.

Make sure you check out both Murray Towns Brewing Co. and The Woolhouse on their socials.
