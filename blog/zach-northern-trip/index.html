Winter getaways have often been limited to a single weekend in Melbourne, or day trip to the Victorian High Country. Canberra has also crept in as of late as a great destination. I’m sure the mention of these locations will have your brain running wild about all the incredible breweries one can attend on the journey! For my family and I, heading further North hasn't been at the top of the priority list during the colder months, despite yours truly believing himself to be a bit of a sunchaser. So we set out to change that, by using the mid-year school holiday break to travel to the Gold Coast via some incredibly cool stop over towns.


As a passionate craft beer consumer, balancing the needs of kids with my own interests can be a bit of a tricky task. Generally, before approaching my better half with a lunch, dinner or 'quick schooner' request, I run it through a set of rules.
1. Do they have a kids menu?
2. Is there a play area, or games?
3. Do they brew something other than beer my wife might enjoy


As you will learn from the following recount, despite all good intentions, those questions don't always end up being answered with an unequivocal yes, however we were certainly blessed to have nothing but positive experiences and fun stories to tell about our trip.


Check the episode out on YouTube, Spotify, or Apple Podcasts.

Make sure you check out both Murray Towns Brewing Co. and The Woolhouse on their socials.
