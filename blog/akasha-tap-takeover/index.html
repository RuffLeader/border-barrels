On a special episode of Border Barrels, the boys head across the mighty Murray to the home of craft beer on the border, The Goods Shed, for their Akasha Tap Takeover event. This one has, interviews (wow!) with Dan from The Goods Shed, and Larry from Akasha Brewing. It has all of us trying some delicious craft beer (some exclusive to the event), and as always, it has some terrible banter from the boys. This is the inaugural Beercast, this is our most fun pod to date, so get around it! We would love to do an infinite more of these types of pods.

Make sure you check out both Akasha and The Goods Shed on their socials.
